How to Play:

Navigate through the map with "WASD"

Open your Inventory with "i"

On the map you can see the structure of the room you are in

However strange objects Will appear with a "?"

Go to those objects to take a picture of them

Some objects can be items, puzzles or monsters...

You can try to combine items and use them to complete puzles

If you are in danger you can either try to run, try begging for mercy or fight

Open the locked door inside Room 7

Escape!
